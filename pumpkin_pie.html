Step 1: Cook the pumpkin and smash it into mud

Step 2: Add sugar, low-gluten flour and an egg to the softened butter, and knead it for 20 minutes

Step 3: Add sugar, two eggs and whipped cream to the smashed pumpkin

Step 4: Take out the dough, put it into the mold, and then put the smashed pumpkin on top of it
